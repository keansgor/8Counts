FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Should I set up a Sdn Bhd (Malaysia Private Limited Company) ?
The summarization of the advantage and disadvantage of Sdn Bhd are :-

ADVANTAGE
1. Separate legal entity and owner’s wealth will be protected
2. Easier to expand the business and raising funds in future
3. Exposed to lower income tax risk (enjoy lower tax rate)
4. Able to transfer the ownership and saleable

DISADVANTAGE
Higher maintenance cost compare to other business vehicle. However, it will worth every penny and effort spend due to the lower income tax risk and separate legal entity which able to protect the owner of the business if business goes wrong (ie: get sued or bankrupt).
What are the requirement to incorporate Sdn Bhd ?
The requirements to form a Sdn Bhd or private limited company are:
1. A minimum of one director
2. A minimum of one shareholder
3. A company secretary who is certified and licensed under Company Commission of Malaysia (SSM)

The whole process will take around x-x working days.
However, the incorporate time is dependent to the completeness of the documents & information.
Any requirement to become a Director in Sdn Bhd ?
The requirements of the person need to be :-
1. Natural Person
2. Minimum aged of 18 and above
3. He or she is not bankrupt and imprisoned
4. He/She is not convicted whether within or outside Malaysia of any offence.
